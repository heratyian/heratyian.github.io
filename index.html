Learning resources

Published: Fri 04 March 2016
By Ian

In Blog.

I made a personal goal 2.5 years ago to ship a game to the iOS app store. I had never studied computer science before. I am really proud of GoChickenGo. I will continue to improve it and learn more about computer science. Here is a list of resources I found incredibly helpful along the way.

Harvard CS50

This class is really popular amongst Harvard students. It is a great introduction to computer science and will help you no matter what you want to do. There is also a reddit page that I found helpful.

Make School

This is a good resource for students who have already completed an introductory computer science course and want to start building apps. Make School emphasizes learning by doing and shipping apps.

Treehouse

Treehouse has a large collection of resources on many different technology topics. It is great for beginners.

Udacity

Udacity is similar to Treehouse in some respects, except it's free! You can also pay extra for certification programs.
